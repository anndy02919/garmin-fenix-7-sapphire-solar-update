How to Update Garmin Fenix 7 Sapphire Solar Maps

You can update your Garmin Fenix 7 Sapphire Solar using Garmin Express (via USB) or directly over Wi-Fi for convenience.

Method 1: Update with Garmin Express

  1. Download and install Garmin Express on your computer.
  2. Connect your Fenix 7 to your computer using the charging cable.
  3. Launch Garmin Express and add your device if prompted.
  4. The software will detect available updates, including maps.
  5. Select "Install All" to update both maps and firmware.

Method 2: Update Over Wi-Fi

  1. Ensure your device is connected to a reliable Wi-Fi network.
  2. Go to Settings > Wi-Fi on your watch and connect.
  3. Navigate to Settings > System > Software Update.
  4. Download and install available updates directly to the watch.

Troubleshooting Update Issues

If you experience issues during a map update, try these steps:

  • Ensure your watch is at least 50% charged before beginning.
  • Use an official Garmin cable and USB 3.0 port for best performance.
  • Restart your device and PC before retrying updates via Garmin Express.
  • Re-add your watch in Garmin Express if it's not recognized.
